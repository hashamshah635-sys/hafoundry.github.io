Privacy Policy

LingoBridge by HaFoundry  ·  Last updated: June 22, 2026

1. What We Collect

When you use LingoBridge, we collect the following information:

  • Email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Password — stored securely as a bcrypt hash; we never store plain-text passwords
  • Message content — text you choose to translate is sent to our backend for translation and is not stored or logged
  • Voice audio — if you use the voice transcription feature (Pro/Unlimited plans), audio is sent to our transcription server and immediately discarded after transcription
  • Usage data — number of messages translated, plan type, and plan expiry date

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide real-time translation and transcription services
  • To manage your subscription plan and usage limits
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. subscription confirmation)

3. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data to any third party
  • We do not store the content of your translated messages
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not share your data with anyone except the services listed below

4. Third-Party Services

  • Paddle — handles payment processing. Card details never reach our servers. See Paddle's Privacy Policy.
  • LibreTranslate — powers translation. Text is sent for translation and not stored.
  • Render.com — hosts our backend servers.
  • Resend — used to send transactional emails.

5. Data Storage

Your account data (email, hashed password, plan info) is stored on our backend server hosted on Render.com in the EU (Frankfurt region). We do not store message content or audio files.

6. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
